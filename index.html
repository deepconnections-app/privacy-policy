Privacy Policy

Effective: March 6, 2026 · Version 1.1.0

Deep Connections is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe your conversations are yours alone.

Overview

Deep Connections ("the App") is a conversation starter app designed to foster meaningful real-world conversations. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Information We Collect

  • Anonymous device identifier — generated locally on your device, not linked to your identity
  • Purchase and subscription status — managed securely by Apple App Store or Google Play via RevenueCat; we do not process or store payment details
  • Anonymous usage analytics — event names (e.g., "mode_started: debate") and interaction counts, with no personally identifiable information; you can opt out in Settings
  • Optional onboarding survey responses — stored locally on your device and optionally in Firebase Firestore (anonymous, linked to device ID only)
  • Optional session feedback — text you voluntarily submit, stored in Firebase Firestore with your anonymous device ID
  • Notification preferences — stored locally on your device
  • Crash reports — anonymous diagnostic data sent to Sentry in production builds only

Information We Do Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or personal contact information
  • Your conversations, answers, or responses to questions shown in the App
  • Precise or coarse location data
  • Photos, contacts, or other device content
  • Advertising identifiers or cross-app tracking data

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the App experience
  • To process purchases and restore premium access
  • To understand usage patterns and prioritize feature development (anonymous analytics)
  • To diagnose and fix technical issues (anonymous crash reports)
  • To deliver locally scheduled push notifications (if enabled by you)
  • To facilitate optional multi-device room sessions

Data Storage and Security

  • Most app data is stored locally on your device using encrypted storage (SecureStore on native, AsyncStorage on web)
  • Firebase Realtime Database is used for optional real-time room sync — room data is temporary and deleted when the room closes
  • Firebase Firestore stores anonymous analytics events, optional survey responses, and optional feedback submissions
  • All server-side data is associated with anonymous device IDs only — never with personal identifiers
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect data in transit and at rest

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • RevenueCat (revenuecat.com) — manages in-app purchases and subscriptions; payment processing is handled entirely by Apple or Google
  • Firebase by Google (firebase.google.com) — provides optional real-time database for room sync, Firestore for analytics and survey storage, and optional authentication
  • Sentry (sentry.io) — collects anonymous crash reports and performance data in production builds only
  • Expo (expo.dev) — development framework; may collect anonymous build and update metadata

Data Sharing and Disclosure

  • We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data with third parties
  • We do not serve advertisements or use ad-tracking technologies
  • We may share anonymous, aggregated usage statistics that cannot identify individual users
  • We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or legal process

Children's Privacy

The App is rated 17+ due to mature content in the Couples Flames mode. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 17. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will take steps to remove it.

Your Rights and Choices

  • Opt out of analytics — toggle "Usage analytics" off in Settings > Privacy at any time
  • Opt out of notifications — disable in Settings or in your device's notification settings
  • Delete all local data — uninstall the App to remove all locally stored data
  • Request data deletion — contact us to request deletion of any data associated with your anonymous device ID from our servers
  • Restore purchases — available anytime through the App without creating an account
  • Access your data — contact us to request a copy of any data associated with your device ID

Data Retention

  • Local data persists until you uninstall the App or clear app data
  • Room session data is temporary and deleted when a room closes
  • Analytics events are retained for up to 24 months for trend analysis, then deleted
  • Feedback submissions are retained until the associated feature improvement is completed or upon your deletion request
  • Crash reports are retained for 90 days

International Data Transfers

If you use the App outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any cross-border data transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this policy and, where appropriate, through an in-app notification. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
